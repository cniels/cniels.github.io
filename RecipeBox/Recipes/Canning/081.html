Quick Pepper Jelly

Spicy jely poured over cream cheese makes an instant hors d'oeuvre spread.

3/4 c. apple jelly (10-oz jar)
1/2 to 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (or go for more pepper flakes - to your taste)

Combine jelly + pepper flakes in small saucepan. Cook, stirring, over low heat until melted, 5 min. Transfer to small bowl. Cover and refrigerate 2 hrs or o'nite.
