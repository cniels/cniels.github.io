(From Jenna's site) Lime Pickles
Aunt B

7 lbs pickles, slice round
Soak 24 hours in 2 gal water & 2 cups slack lime. Rinse several times.
Soak 3 hrs in cold water then pour off.
2 qts vinegar, 4 1/2 lbs sugar 1 ts. celery salt, 1 t whole clover
1 T pickling spice, 1 T salt
Let stand overnight in this solution, then boil pickles in solution 35 min.
Can & seal (Lime at lumber yard)
