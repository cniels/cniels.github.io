Apple Butter

I cut sugar 1/2
10-05

8 medium apples (any cooking apple), quartered and cored
1 cup apple cider or apple juice
1 1/4 cups sugar
3/5 cup brown sugar
1 to 2 teaspoons cinnamon, or to taste
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

Combine apples and apple juice in a 2-quart casserole. Microwave, covered, on high for 8 to 10 minutes or until apples are soft. Stir twice. Transfer to blender container. Process on medium speed until apples are of desired smoothness. return to casserole. Stir in sugar, cloves and cinnamon. Microwave on high, uncovered, 10 to 12 minutes or until thickened. Stir twice. Makes about 4 cups.

-Jane Serfling
