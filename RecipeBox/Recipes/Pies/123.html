Rhubarb Crumb

1 cup sugar
1 cup sifted flour
Sift together

1 tsp. baking pd.
1/2 cup shorting cut in shorting
1 egg add egg and salt
dash of salt
4 cups sliced rhubarb (fresh)
mix with 1 cup sugar

Put fruit mixture in 8x8" greased pan. Crumble topping over it
Bake 350° about 45 min.
Serve hot or cold with or without cream or icecream.
