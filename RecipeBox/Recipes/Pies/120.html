Better Than Sex Dessert

1 c. flour
1/2 c. melted oleo
1/2 c. chopped nuts
mix together - bake 350° for 15 min

Beat together 1 8oz cream cheese
1 c. sugar

Add: 1 9oz cool whip. Spread on crust
Mix: 2 small pkgs. instant pudding (any flavor) as pkg. directs. Spread again. Top with Cool Whip.
