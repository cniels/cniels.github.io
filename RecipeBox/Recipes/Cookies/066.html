Lillian Malouff's Oatmeal Cookies

3 eggs, beaten
1 cup raisins
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup shortening (I used butter-flavored)
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 1/2 cups flour
2 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

1. Beat the eggs and add the cup of raisins and teaspoon of vanilla. Allow to sit for one hour.
2. Heat oven to 350.
3. Cream shortening and sugars, sift dry ingredients together.
4. Add eggs, raisins, vanilla mixture to shortening and sugar. Mix
5. Add flour, salt, soda mixture. Stir together with wet ingredients.
6. Add and mix in oatmeal, then walnuts. Dough will be very stiff.
7. Drop cookies, about a rounded teaspoonful each (sometimes easier to roll in balls) on ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes at 350.
