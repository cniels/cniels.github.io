Chocolate Chip and Peanut Cookies

1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup Safeway All Purpose Flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 6 ounce package (1 cup) semisweet chocolate pieces
1 cup peanuts

Preheat oven to 375°F.
Cream butter or margarine and sugars; add egg, vanilla and beat until light and fluffy. Sift together flour, salt and baking soda; stir into creamed mixture; blend well. Add chocolate pieces and peanuts and stir by hand.
Drop from teaspoon about 2 inches apart on a greased cookie sheet. Bake in 375°F oven 12-14 minutes. Remove from sheet immediately.
Yield: 3 dozen cookies.
