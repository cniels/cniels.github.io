Oatmeal Crispies

Beth Rush

1 1/4 C. Shortening
1 C. Sugar
1 C. brown Sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 C. flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. soda
3 C. oatmeal uncooked
1 C. chopped nuts (or 1/2 C. Grape Nuts)

Cream shortening, gradually add sugars, then eggs and vanilla, flour, salt, baking soda. Blend well. Add oatmeal and nuts. Drop by tsp. on ungreased cookie sheet.
Oven 350° Bake 15-18 min.
11 min - Don't speed
