Chocolate Cherry Bars

Paula Jean Spreadborough of Kelso, Washington promised that her dark, moist cherry-studded bars would be perfect for a potluck or a take-to-school treat...and she was right!

Cake:

1 chocolate fudge cake mix
1 can (21 ounces) cherry pie filling
1 teaspoon almond extract
2 eggs, beaten

Fudge Glaze

5 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup milk
1 cup (6 ounces) semisweet chocolate chips

Mix cake ingredients together by hand (batter will be stiff). Bake at 350° for 30 minutes in greased and floured 13- x 9-in. baking pan (325° if you use glass). Cool 10 minutes. Make glaze by combining butter, sugar and milk in small saucepan. Bring to boil; boil 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in chips until smooth. Spread over still-warm cake. Yield: 3 dozen bars.
