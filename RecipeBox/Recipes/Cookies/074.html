Date Cookies

Simmer slowly in saucepan until melted 2 cubes of oleo, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 lb. chopped nuts dates, 1/2 tsp. vanilla, 1 beaten egg. Stir together. Boil 2 min. Remove; add 2 cups chopped nuts, 2 cups rice Krispies; when cool enough to handle roll into balls and then into coconut.
