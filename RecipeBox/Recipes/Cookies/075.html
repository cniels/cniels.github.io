Sugar Cookies

1 cup Butter (can use part oleo)
1 1/4 cup powdered sugar
1 Beaten egg

Sift together:
2 1/4 cup sifted flour
pinch salt
1/2 tsp. cream of tarter
1/2 tsp soda

1 tsp. vanilla

Cream butter with powdered sugar. Add egg. Then add flour mixture + vanilla.

Chill dough.
Roll in Balls - walnut size
Flatten with glass dipped in sugar
Bake at 375° for 10-12 minutes.
