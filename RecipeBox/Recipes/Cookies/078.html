Mollasses Sugar Cookies

3/4 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup molasses
1 egg
2 cups sifted flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. salt

over

Cream together shortening and sugar. Add molasses and egg; Beat well; Mix together flour, soda, cinnamon, cloves, ginger and salt; add to first mixture. Mix well; chill. Form 1" balls, roll in granulated sugar and place on greased cookie sheets two inches apart. Bake in moderately hot oven. (350°) 8-10 min.
