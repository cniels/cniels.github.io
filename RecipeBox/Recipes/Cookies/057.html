Pumpkin Cookies (Alane)

Cream:
1/2 cup shortning
1 cup sugar
1 cup pumpkin

Add:
1 cup raisens
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup nuts

Stir in:
2 cups flour
1 tsp each of: baking powder, soda, and cinnamon

Bake at 350° for 14 minutes
