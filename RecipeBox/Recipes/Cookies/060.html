Peppernuts

1 cup dark syrup OR 1/2 cup syrup + 1/2 cup sorghum
1 cup sugar OR (1/2 cup honey + 1/2 cup sugar)
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 cup shortening
1/8 tsp. cloves
2 eggs
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. soda
1/4 tsp anise oil or 1/2 tsp. anise seed
5 cups flour

Place syrup, sugar, shortening and honey into sauce pan and heat the remaining ingredients. Mix well and cool overnight. Roll into long small rolls and cool again. Cut with a sharp knife and bake on a greased cooky sheet in a moderately hot oven 375° 12 to 15 min.
The cookies when baked should be about the size of a nickel.
[11 min]
