PB Jam Cookies

350° 10 - 12 min

Melt 2 cubes butter
Stir in - 1/2 cup white sugar
            2 c brown sugar
            1 1/2 tsp soda
            1 tsp. salt
            1 tsp baking powder
            1 cup peanut butter
Add 2 slightly beaten eggs
Stir in 3 c flour, packed
            1 c chopped peanuts

Make walnut balls
Drop tsp full dough on Pam sprayed pan
Make indentation - fill hole with jam
Cool after baking/remove from pan
