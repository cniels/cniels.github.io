Oatmeal Cookies

Combine, let stand 1 hr:
3 eggs well Beaten
1 C. raisins
1 tsp. vanilla

Cream:
1 C. Shortening
1 C. brown sugar
1 C. granulated sugar

Sift into sugar mixture:
2 1/2 C. sifted flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp soda
1 tsp cinnamon

Blend into egg mixture:
2 C. oatmeal
1/2 C. chopped nuts

Bake on ungreased pan in oven at 350° for 10-12 min.
