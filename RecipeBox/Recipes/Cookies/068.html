Charlene's Recipe Box

No Bake Chocolate Cookies

2 c. sugar
1/2 c. milk
1 stick butter or oleo
3 T. cocoa
1 tsp salt
Put into pan, bring to boil.
Remove from heat. Add:
1 tsp. vanilla
3 cups rolled oats
1/2 c. broken walnuts
1 c. coconut
Drop from tsp. onto wax paper. - 18 cookies
