Almond Crunch

Eddy Sheaves

12 double graham crackers
2 sticks butter
1/2 c. sugar
4 oz. sliced almonds or 1 c. chopped pecans

Line cookie sheet with foil. Place graham crackers on foil. Sprinkle nuts over crackers. Bring butter + sugar to boiling point in heavy pan. Boil 2 1/2 min. Pour over crackers + nuts. Bake for 10-12 min.

Let cool - break into pieces.
(Note: This burns easily; so watch carefully - may not need required baking time)
Eddy Sheaves cuts with pizza cutter
