Rice Crispy Cookies

1 C B Sugar
1 C W Sugar
1 C shortning
2 eggs
2 C Flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tes B Powder
1 tes Soda
2 C oatmeal
1 tes vanilla
1 C Rice Crispy or coconut
1 C Choc. Chips

Cream shortning. Blend in sugar + eggs. Sift dry ingredients together. Add to first mixture. Stir in vanilla, oatmeal, crispy + choc. chips. Mix well drop

Rice Crispy Cookies (Mother's)

1 c. shortening
1 c. white sugar
1 c. brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 c. flour
1/2 tsp. salt, 1 tsp. soda, 1 tsp. baking powder
2 c. oatmeal
1 c. choc. chips
1 c. Rice Crispies or coconut
350° - about 12 minutes
