Rice Crispy Cookies

Grandmother Heatwole

1 cup brown sugar
1 cup shortening
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 cup flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cup oatmeal
1 cup rice crispy or coconut
1 cup choc. chips
1 tsp. baking soda

(To large to double
9-99 cn.)

Cream shortening blend in sugar and eggs. Sift dry ingredients together. Add to first mixture. Stir in vanilla, oatmeal and crispy and chocolate chips. Mix well. Drop on greased tin. Bake 10 min. at 350°. (I used a little less flour and oatmeal. For they are stiff. I make little balls and press them down.)

I baked cookies for 12 minutes
