Make Ahead Scrambled Eggs

Montine

3 T. flour
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1 1/2 cups milk
1 dozen eggs
1/2 cup margarine
shredded cheese, chopped ham
diced chilies, etc. whatever

over

Beat together flour, salt and pepper and milk until smooth. Beat in eggs, mixing well. Melt margarine in large skillet over moderate heat. Add egg mixture. Cook until eggs are done, stirring frequently. When eggs are almost done, add optional ingredients. Eggs may be kept hot two hours or longer in chafing dish or 300° oven. Leftover eggs may be refrigerated and reheated.
