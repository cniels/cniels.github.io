Skillit Enchiladas

1 lb. g. beef
1 lg. chopped onion
1 C. cream of mushroom soup
1 can. Enchilada sauce (10 oz old Elpaso)
1/3 C. milk
3 small chilles or (1 can canned chopped chillis)
1 pkg. soft tortillas
2 1/2 C. shredded colby cheese

Brown meat - pepper + 1/2 onion + cook, drain. Add soup, sauce, milk, peppers. Simmer on low till all well mixed.

Fry shells in seconds - Drain.
Fill shells with grated cheese + chopped onion. Roll up + place in simmered sauce flap side down. Cover + simmer 10-15 min.
