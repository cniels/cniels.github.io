Chicken a la King

In a saucepan melt 1/4 c. butter (oleo or chicken fat) blend in 1/3 c. Flour, 1/2 tsp. Salt
Add 1 c. chicken broth and 1 c. milk.
Cook, stirring constantly till sauce is thick + bubbly. Add c. diced chicken, one 3 oz. can mushrooms drained + 1/4 c. drained canned pimento. Heat thoroughly. Serve over toast.
