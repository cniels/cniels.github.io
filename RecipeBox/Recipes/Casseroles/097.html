Broccoli Casserole

2 pkg. frozen broccoli - chopped and cooked 5 min.
1 can mushroom soup
2 T maymaise
3 eggs - beaten
1 cup Ritz crackers - crushed for topping
1 cup grated cheese

Mix thoroughly (except crumbs) Bake at 350 for 35 min. Cover with a little egg, top with crackers dot with butter for 10 min.
