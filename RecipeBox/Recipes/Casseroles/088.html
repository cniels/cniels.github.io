Ham and Cheese Strata

12 slices white bread, trimmed
3/4 lb sharp cheese, sliced
1 pkg. frozen chopped broccoli, cooked, drained
2 cups cooked diced ham
Mix together:
6 slightly beaten eggs
3 1/2 cups milk
2 T. minced onion
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. dry mustard
1 cup grated cheese
over

Cut bread into triangles. Grease 9 x 13 pan, line with bread, cheese slices, broccoli, ham and egg mix. Refrigerate overnight. Bake, uncovered for 55 minutes in 325° oven. Sprinkle grated cheese on top last five minutes.
