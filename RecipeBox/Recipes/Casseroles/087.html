Blackie's Breakfast Casserole

2 or 3 cans (sm) diced green chiles
9 - 12 eggs, slightly beaten
1 lb. longhorn cheese, grated
1 lb. Monterrey cheese, grated
1/4 cup onion, diced finely
1/4 cup milk

Layer ingredients 1/3rd each layer - chiles, eggs, cheeses, onion. Pour milk over all. Bake in 350° oven about 45-50 minutes until set.
