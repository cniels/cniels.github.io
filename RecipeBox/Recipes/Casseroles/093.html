Tamala Pie

1 lb. ground beef
1 large chopped onion
1 T. fat
2 T. flour
1 14 1/2 oz. can (1 2/3 cups) tomatoes
1 T. chili powder
1 tsp. salt
3/4 cups yellow corn meal
2 cups boiling water
1 tsp. salt

over

Brown beef and onion in fat. Blend in flour; add tomatoes, chili powder, and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Pour into 1 1/2 qt. casserole. Sprinkle corn meal into salted boiling water; cook until thickened, stirring constantly. Drop by Tablespoons over ground beef mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
