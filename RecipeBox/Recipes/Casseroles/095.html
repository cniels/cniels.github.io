Chicken-Rice Casserole

Grease 9x13" baking dish. Spread 3/4 cup rice over bottom. Lay pieces of chicken on top of rice.
Mix:
1 cup onion soup
1 cup golden mushroom soup
1 cup water
1/3 cup Jalopeno Sauce
Pour the mix over chicken and rice. Bake 1 1/2 hrs. 350°
