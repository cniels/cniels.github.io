Enchilada Casserole

Brown 1 1/2 lb. ground beef, drain fat and add:
1 can cream cheese soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cans (4 1/2 oz. each) Taco Sauce.

Simmer a few minutes.
Combine:
1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion

Cut into quarter 12 soft tortillas.
Line a greased 12 x 7 bakking dish with half the tortillas. Pour half of beef mixture over tortillas. Sprinkle with half cheese mixture. Cover with remaining tortillas. Repeat beef and cheese layers. Bake at 350° for 25-30 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before serving.
