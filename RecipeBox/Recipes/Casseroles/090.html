Deviled Tuna and Macaroni

4 oz. shell macaroni
2 T. butter or margarine
1 T. flour
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. paprika
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 cup milk
1 T. lemon juice
1 cup flaked tuna (17)
2 hard cooked eggs
1 T. chopped peminto
sliced stuffed olives

cook macaroni in boiling salted water until tender - about 8 min. Drain and rinse - while macaroni is cooking melt butter in sauce pan. Stir in flour, salt, paprika, and Worcestershire sauce. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until slightly thickened. Add lemon juice and tuna. Dice hard cooked eggs reserving yolks for garnish. Fold eggs and macaroni into deviled sauce. Mix thoroughly. Pour into greased baking dish. Bake in moderate oven 350° for 20 min. Garnish with deviled egg yolk, pimiento and olives. Makes 4 servings.
