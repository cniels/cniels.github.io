Applesauce Fruitcake

INGREDIENTS
For a 9-inch, 6-cup loaf pan:
2 cups golden seedless raisins
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup each: brown sugar and white sugar
1 teaspoon each: ground cloves, cinnamon, allspice, salt
1 cup hazelnuts or walnuts
1 tablespoon baking powder
4 tablespoons butter, melted
1 cup cool, thick applesauce
1 Toss the raisins in the flour, add the sugars, seasonings, nuts, and baking powder. Beat the butter into the thick applesauce; blend into the flour. [over]

2 Turn batter into a buttered and floured pan, its bottom lined with wax paper. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 300°, or until a skewer comes out clean - do not press or poke until set, or cake may deflate. Cool 20 minutes in the pan, then unmold, turn right-side up and cool on a rack. Wrap and let cure a day before serving, as is or with vanilla cream.
