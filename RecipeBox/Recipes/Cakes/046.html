Pumpkin Cheesecake

Crust:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/3 cup melted butter
3 tablespoons sugar
Mix crumbs with butter and sugar; press into springform pan; bake 1 minutes at 350 degrees.
32 ounces cream cheese
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 can pumpkin
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
Beat cream cheese until smooth; add remaining ingredients. Spoon over crust and bake at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes. Let cook and refrigerate at least 24 hours.

Pumpkin Bundt Cake

2 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking soda
3 teaspoons cinnamon
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1/2 cup oil
2 cups pumpkin
Sift flour, salt, soda and cinnamon. Set aside. Cream sugar, eggs and oil. Add pumpkin to creamed mixture. Add dry ingredients and mix well. Pour into greased and floured bundt pan. Bake in 325-degree oven for 45-6 minutes or until done. Cool in pan 10-15 minutes. Remove from pan and cool completely. Frost with cream cheese icing.
Cream cheese icing:
1/4 cup melted butter or margarine
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup chopped nuts
Combine frosting ingredients and spread on cooled cake. Sprinkle with nuts.

Pumpkin Orange Cake

1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 1/4 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 cup canned pumpkin
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon grated orange peel
2 cups all-purpose flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. In another mixing bowl, beat pumpkin, orange juice, milk and orange peel. Combine dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately with pumpkin mixture. Fold in nuts. Pour into a greased 13 by 9 by 2-inch baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack.
Frosting:
1/3 cup butter or margarine, softened
3 cups confectioners' sugar
3 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons orange juice
1 tablespoon grated orange peel
Candied orange peel, optional
For frosting, combine butter and confectioners' sugar in a mixing bowl. Beat in the milk, orange juice and peel. Frost cake. Garnish with candied peel if desired.
