Lemon Cake

1 lemon cake mix without pudding
4 eggs
1 pkg instant pudding mix
3 T flour
2/3 cup salad oil
1 1/2 c. water
Mix above 4 min. Pour into a 9x13 pan + bae at 350° for 40 min. Mix 1 (6oz) can of frozen lemonade with 2 c. powdered sugar. When cake is done, take out of oven and poke (over)

holes all over with a fork + spread lemonade mixture over + let soak in.
Serve hot or cold. Also good with strawberries + cream.
