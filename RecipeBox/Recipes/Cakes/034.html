Beth Rush's Family Raisin Cake

325° - 60 min

3/4 c. shortening
1 1/2 c. sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp each: cinnamon + nutmeg
1/2 tsp. alspice
1 c. raisin juice (water to plump raisins)
with 1 tsp soda
2 c. raisins
3 c. flour + nuts
(Don't o'mix)
Cream [first three] together + add dry ing. with raisins
