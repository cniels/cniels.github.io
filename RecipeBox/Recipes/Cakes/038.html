Double Orange Delight

Combining two mixes cuts the mixing time in half.
Cake:
1 yellow cake mix
1 package instant lemon pudding mix
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup hot water
4 eggs
1 tablespoon lemon extract
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
Glaze:
1 cup fresh orange juice
2 cups sugar
grated peel of one orange
Cake: Preheat oven to 325°. Grease and flour a tube cake pan of any kind. Combine all of the cake ingredients in a bowl and beat 4 minutes. Pour into pan and bake 45 minutes or until done. While still hot, pour glaze over it.
Glaze: Combine ingredients in saucepan and bring to a boil. After pouring over cake, let cake sit until glaze is entirely absorbed, then remove cake from pan.

Orange Fruit Cake - Lenna

1 c shortning
2 c sugar
2 T grated rind
1 1/2 c buttermilk
1 t soda
4 c flour
4 eggs, beaten
2 pkg dates (or 1 pk dates + 1 c raisins)
1 c nuts
Bake 1 hr - 1/2 hr at 300° - 1/2 hr at 350°
Sauce
1 c orange juice
2 c sugar
2 T orange rind
Combine + heat until dissolved. Pour while warm on hot cake in pan. Let cool then remove.
Bake in tube pan
