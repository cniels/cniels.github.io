Party Cake (Rum flavored)

350°- 35-40 min
9"x13"pan

1 yellow cake mix
1 pkg. instant vanilla pudding
4 eggs
1/2 c. oil
3/4 c. water
2 T. vinegar
1 tsp. rum flavoring

Pour batter in pan over 1/2 c. chopped nuts. Bake. After baking, poke holes in cake. Pour over the

following glaze:
1/4 c. sugar
1/3 c. oleo
1/4 c. milk
heated + stirred, cooked 2 min

Add 1 tsp. rum flavoring
