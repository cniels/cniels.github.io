Spicy Whipped Cream

Cookbook: 1st Presbyterian Church Albuquerque

1/2 c. packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg
3/4 c. whipping cream
1/2 tsp vanilla
Mix sugar, spices in med bowl. Stir in whipping cream + vanilla. Chill 1 hr., then whip until stiff.
