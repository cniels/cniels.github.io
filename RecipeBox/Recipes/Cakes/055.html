Sheet Cake

400° - 20 min
Mom's

Combine in a large bowl:
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. soda

Combine in pan and bring to a rapid boil:
1 stick oleo
1/2 c. shortening
4 T. cocoa
1 c. water
Pour over the flour sugar mixture and beat until smooth. Add 1/2 cup buttermilk, 2 eggs, slightly beaten, and 1 tsp. vanilla
over

Mix well. Pour into 10 x 11" pan. Bake 20 min at 400°.
Cover with frosting while cake is hot.
Melt 1/2 stick oleo
2 T. cocoa
3 T. milk - bring to boil and add powdered sugar and 1/2 tsp. vanilla. Add nuts or sprinkle with nuts.
