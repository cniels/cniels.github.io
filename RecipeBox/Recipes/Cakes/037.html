Lemon Poppy Seed Bread

18.25 oz pkg lemon cake mix
3.4 oz pkg instant lemon pudding
1 cup water
1/2 cup oil
4 large eggs
1 T. poppy seeds
2 T. sugar -                 -To combine +
1/2 c. lemon juice      -brush over hot loaves
(over)

Combine cake mix, pudding, water, oil + eggs. Beat at med. speed. Stir in poppy seeds. Pour into 2 greased + floured loaf pans.
Bake @ 325° for 40 min or until done.
Brush lemon juice/sugar mix over hot loaves.
