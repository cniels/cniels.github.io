Ava Jean's Apple Sauce Cake

375° - 35 min.

Mix in order given:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup applesauce, heated
2 tsp. soda disolved in 1/4 cup water
1 cup nuts (walnuts)
1 cup shortening
2 eggs
1 cup rasens
Dry ingredients which are sifted together.
2 1/4 cup flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cloves
1 tsp. cinnamon
