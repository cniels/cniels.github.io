Amazin' Raisin Cake

3 cups unsifted flour
2 cups sugar
2 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cloves
1 cup HELLMANN'S or BEST FOODS Real, Light or Cholesterol Free Reduced Calorie Mayonnaise
1/3 cup milk
2 eggs
3 cups coarsely chopped apples
1 cup raisins
1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
1 cup heavy or whipping cream, whipped
Grease and flour two (9-inch) layer cake pans. In large bowl combine flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and cloves. Add mayonnaise, milk and eggs. Beat at low speed 2 minutes, scraping bowl frequently. (Batter will be thick.) With spoon, stir in apples, raisins and nuts. Spoon into pans. Bake in 350°F oven 40 to 45 minutes or until cake tester inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pans 10 minutes. Remove; cool on racks. Fill and frost with whipped cream. Makes 12 servings.
