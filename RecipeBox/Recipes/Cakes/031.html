Date Nut Cake

Pauline Nielsen

2 C. sugar
1/2 C. Butter or oleo
(cream together)
add 2 eggs + Tbs. Vanilla Beat together
add alternately - 2 C. sour milk or buttermilk
3 C. flour
2 tsp. soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. cloves
add 2 C. chopped nuts
1 lb chopped Dates (over)

Bake 350° - 1 hr -
or until pick comes clean
line baking pans with waxed paper or brown paper bag
