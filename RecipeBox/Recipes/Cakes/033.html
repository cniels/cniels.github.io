Rum Cake

Pauline Nielsen

1 c. [1/2] chopped pecans [-Pan bottom]
1 pkg. instant Vanilla Pudding
1/2 C cold water
1/2 C dark rum [1/4 C]
1 pkg yellow cake mix
4 Eggs
1/2 C oil
[1/4 finely chopped pecans]
Grease + flour bundt cake pan. Sprinkle nuts on bottom. Mix all other ingredients well. Pour into pan. Bake 325° for 1 hr. [50-60 min] Remove from oven.
(over)

While cake still warm, pour on glaze.

Rum Glaze

1/4 lb butter [1/2 c margarine]
1 C. sugar
1/4 C water
[1/4 C.] 1/2 C. dark rum [cook about 3 min]
Melt butter in sauce pan. Stir in water + sugar. Boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat + stir in rum. Cool glaze a bit + pour over warm cake + let it absorb for about 3 min. Remove from pan. decorate with whipped cream + cherries
