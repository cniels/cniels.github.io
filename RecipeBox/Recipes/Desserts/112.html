Banana Split Dessert

1 1/2 pkg. graham crackers, crushed
1/2 c. melted oleo (or fat free oleo)
1 pkg. vanilla (sugar free) instant pudding
1 3/4 c. milk
1 lg. can drained pineapple
2-4 sliced bananas
2 ? Tubs fat free Cool Whip
optional - chopped nuts on top

Spread oleo + crushed crackers in 9x13" pan. Bake 10 min 350°.
Cool.

Mix pudding - mik - spread on crumbs when set.
Top with pineapple - then bananas - then cool whip.
Refrigerate
Abt 4 pts 4x4" square
