Pear Pie (or other fruit)

1/3 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. lemon juice
1/4 - 1/2 cup milk
Mix together

Cream or half and half on top (1/4 c. +)
450 for 10 min. 350 1/2 hr. additionally
(use pre-cooked apples)
