Cranberry Delight

Submitted by Ruby Glasco

Combine:
1 12-ounce or 16-ounce package of cranberries, ground.
1 cup sugar
1 flat can crushed pineapple

Mix well and refrigerate over night. The following day, prepare one package Dream Whip or one small Cool Whip.

Combine with cranberry mixture:
1 cup mini marshmallows
1 cup chopped nuts, optional

Refrigerate several hours before serving. This dessert will compliment any meal, especially during the holiday season.
