Christmas Pudding

2 sticks oleo
2 cups flour
2 cups nuts
Melt oleo in 9x12 pan. Add flour and nuts. Bake at 350° for 15 minutes. Cool Cut flour mix in half

16 oz. cream cheese
2 cups powdered sugar
2 cups Cool Whip
Mix cream cheese with beater until smooth. Add powdered sugar. Fold in Cool Whip. Spread on crust.

2 large pkg. vanilla instant pudding
Red and green food coloring
6 cups milk
4 cups Cool Whip
Divide pudding into 2 bowls. Beat 1 pkg pudding and 3 cups milk until thick. Add a few drops red food coloring. Fold in 2 cups Cool Whip. Add this layer on top of cream cheese layer. Repeat process with second pkg. pudding but add green food coloring. Spread 2 cups Cool Whip as final layer. Sprinkle with nuts.
