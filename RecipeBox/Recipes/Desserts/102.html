Charlene's Recipe Box

Apple Desert/Alane

6 apples
6 T flour
1/4 c gran sugar
1/2 tsp. lemon zest
1 T. lemon juice

Bake at 400° - 40 min (To bubbling)

Add CRISP topping:
6 T. flour
1/4 c each of brown sugar & gran sugar
5 T cold butter
1/4 tsp each of salt & cinnamon
Bake 5 min more
