Aunt B's Pumpkin Pie

Bake 450° - 15 min
350° - 40-45 min

3/4 c. sugar
1 T. cornstarch
2 eggs separated
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/2 c. milk
1/4 tsp. ginger
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
dash cloves
3/4 c. pumpkin

Blend sugar, cornstarch + egg yolks together with spices, salt + pumpkin. Gradually add milk. Beat egg whites - fold into pumpkin mix - Turn into pastry shell. Bake until knife inserted in

middle comes out clean.

- crack oven door 4-5 min. after the 450° baking time.
