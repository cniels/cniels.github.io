Moon Pie - Julie Worley

Crust:
Boil together 1 c. water + 1 stick margarine. After this boils, add 1 c. flour + beat well until smooth. When flour mix is smooth, add 4 eggs - one at a time - + beat after you add each egg. Spread in a well greased jelly-roll pan; bake until golden brown at 350° 20-25 min. (crust will look like moon critters)

(over)

Filling:
Cream together 1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese with 1/2 c. milk. When smooth, add 4 more c. milk + mix. To this mix add a total of 12 oz. instant pudding mix (3 regular size pkgs or 2 Large size pkgs). Spread mix over cooled crust.

Frost with 8 oz Cool Whip

Refrigerate until ready to serve

When ready to serve, Drizzle chocolate sauce over top
