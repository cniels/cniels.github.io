Apple Crisp

3 Granny Smith apples
3 McIntosh apples
1 T lemon juice, 1/2 tsp zest
1/4 cup sugar, 6 T flour

Topping
6 T flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp. cinnamon, 1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp. salt, 5 T cold butter

Bake at 400° for 40 minutes.
