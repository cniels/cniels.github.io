Cheese Roll

2 1bx. Velveeta cheese
8 oz. cream cheese
4 cloves fresh garlic, crushed
1 cup pecans, chopped
Chili Powder
Let cheeses reach room temperature-about 4 hours, then mix all the ingredients (except chili powder) by hand in a large bowl until well mixed. Separate into 6 balls or 6 small loaves, roll in chili powder, wrap in waxed paper and refrigerate.
