Cheese Ball

2 Cups grated sharp cheese
1-3 oz package Cream Cheese
Dash of garlic salt
3-5 dashes celery salt
2 tbsp finely grated onions
1/2 tsp worchestershire sauce
3 tablespoon mayonnaise
1/4 cup chopped olives
Chopped pecans or parsley
Mix all ingredients except pecans

or parsley. Chill and make into a ball and roll in chopped pecans or parsley. Serve with assorted crackers.
