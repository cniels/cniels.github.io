7 Layer Bean Dip

1 (29-ounce) can refried beans
1 cup sour cream
1 1/2 cups salsa
1 1/2 cups grated cheddar or monterey jack cheese
1 or 2 large tomatoes, chopped
1 can (8 ounces) black olives, slices or chopped
2 cups chopped lettuce
Layer ingredients in 9 by 13 inch pan
-Rose Davis, CSU Extension
