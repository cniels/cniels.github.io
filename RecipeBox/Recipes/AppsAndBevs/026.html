Cheese Logs

Number of Servings: lots!

2 Pkg. Longhorn Cheese
2 8oz. Pkg. cream cheese
2 T. Worchestershire Sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
6 drops Tabasco Sauce
1. Mix together
2. Roll in Chili powder mixed with paprika
3. Wrap in waxed paper + chill
