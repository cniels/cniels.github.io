Cheese Ball

Caryl Pearce

2 - 8oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. mayonaise
1/2 c. parmesean cheese
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
2 T. minced chopped onion
1/2 tsp. worshestershire sauce
Mix together + roll into a ball. Froll in crushed nuts. ->->

Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.
