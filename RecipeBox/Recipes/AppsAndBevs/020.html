Charlene's Recipe Box

Punch

1 sm. pkg. jello
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 lg. can frozen lemonade
1 42 oz. can pineapple juice
1 qt. ginger ale
Dissolve jello and sugar in 4 cups hot water. Add lemonade and pineapple juice - mix well. Pour into 1 gal. container and freeze.
Thaw punch for about 4 hr. before serving. It should be mushy. Add gingerale just before serving.
Serves 30
