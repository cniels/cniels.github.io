Holiday Pumpkin Bread

350°
50-55 min
2 loaves

3 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp baking pd.
2 tsp soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
Mix together to add to well blended:
2 c. pumpkin
1 c. crisco oil
3 c. sugar
3 eggs
1 c. chopped walnuts
1 c. raisins
Stir in (by hand) (over)

Remove from pan after cooling just 15 min
can be baked in muffin pans -
1/2 c batter - bake 20" - 25" min
optional orange glaze
1 1/2 c. confection sugar
1 tsp orange peel
6 tsp orange juice
