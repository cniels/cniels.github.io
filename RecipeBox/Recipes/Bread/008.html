Dinner Rolls

Ava jean Ewers

soften   2pks yeast in 1/2 c. warm water
mix        1 1/2c. luke warm milk
               1/2 c. sugar
               2 1/2 tsp. salt
               1/2 c. Mazola Oil
               2 eggs, beaten slightly
Add yeast mixture; then add flour to right consistancy (7-7 1/2 c) Let Rise Twice
Make rolls. Let rise. Bake 375° - approx 16 min
