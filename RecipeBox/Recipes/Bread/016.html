Grandmother's Banana Bread

Serves 1 loaf

Cream together: 1/2 cup shortening
                               1 cup sugar
                               2 eggs
Sift together:       2 cups flour
                               1 tsp. soda
                               1 tsp. salt
Add 1 cup mashed bananas to the creamed mixture. Then add dry ingredients. Bake at 350° for 1 hr. or until done. Nuts maybe added. Grandmother sprinkles cinnamon sugar over the top.
