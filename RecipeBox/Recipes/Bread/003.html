Herb Bread Sticks

(Economically made from hot dog buns)

8 hot dog buns
1 teaspoon crushed basil
1/2 cup melted butter or margarine
1 teaspoon parsley flakes
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

Preheat the oven to 300 degrees. Quarter the hot dog buns the long way. Place with cut side up on a large cookie sheet or 9x13-inch cake pan. Combine spices and melted butter; brush onto the cut side of the buns. Bake until crisp and lightly brown, about 1 hour. When completely cooled, store in an air tight container. Makes 32 sticks.
