Stuffing for Ham

4 eggs, separated
6 slices of Bread, cubed
1/2 C margarine
1 lg can pineapple (crushed - drained)
3/4 C sugar
Cream margarine - add sugar, add egg yolks + pineapple. Fold in slightly beaten egg whites. Fold in bread. Bake 45 min to 1 hr @ 350°
Serve beside ham
