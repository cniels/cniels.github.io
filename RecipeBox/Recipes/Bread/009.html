Pizza Dough

Gail Geddes

Let stand 5-min -   3/4 c warm H20
                                   1 pkg yeast
                                   1/4 tsp sugar
Add 1 T. oil
                                   2 cups flour
                                   1/2 tsp salt
Stir with fork
Put oil on pan
Let rest 30 min. Bake 420° 10 mins + 10 min
