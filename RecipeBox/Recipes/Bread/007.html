Cheddar Puff

(cut mustard some CN)

Joyce's Recipe

8 slices white bread (may use crusts)
3 tble. softened butter or margarine
1/2 lb. (2 cups) sharp cheddar cheese, grated
2 1/2 cups milk
2 tble. prepared mustard
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. paprika
4 eggs
Spread bread with butter, cut into large cubes. Place 1/3 cubes in 8-cup baking dish. Sprinkle with 1/3 of cheese; repeat with remaining bread and cheese to make 2 more layers of each. Beat eggs with milk, mustard, salt, Worcestershire, and paprika in bowl; pour over bread-cheese layers; cover. Chill at least 3 hours or overnight. Bake uncovered 350° 1 hr., till puffed and golden. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
