Corn bread casserole

(4-6 servings)

1 can (15-14 oz) whole kernel corn - drained
1 can (14 3/4 oz cream-style corn
1 pkg (8 1/2 oz) cornbread/muffin mix
1 egg
2 T. melted butter or margirine
1/4 tsp. garlic pd.
1/4 tsp. paprika
(over)

Combine all ingredients. Pour into greased 11"x7"x2" pan
Bake uncovered 400° for 25-30 min or until top + edges are golden brown.
