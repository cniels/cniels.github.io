Pumpkin Ribbon Bread

FILLING:
2 packages (3 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup sugar
1 tablespoon flour
2 teaspoons grated orange peel
BREAD
1 cup cooked pumpkin
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1-1/2 cups sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cloves
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1-2/3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup chopped pecans

For filling, beat cream cheese, sugar

and flour together in small bowl. Add egg; mix to blend. Stir in orange peel; set aside. Make bread by combining pumpkin, oil and eggs in large bowl. Add sugar, salt, cloves, cinnamon, flour, baking soda and pecans; mix to blend. Pour one-quarter of batter into two greased and floured 7-1/2-x 3-1/2-x 3-in. loaf pans. Carefully spread the cream cheese mixture over batter. Add remaining batter, covering filling. Bake at 325° for 1-1/2 hours or until bread tests done with wooden pick. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pans. Store in refrigerator.

Meet the Cook
Cathy Van Sickle, Kokomo, Indiana
Pumpkin Ribbon Bread
What I like most about this recipe is the addition of an orange-flavored cream cheese layer to Pumpkin Bread. It's beautiful when cut, with the slices fanned out on a serving plate. You don't have to worry about keeping the cream cheese layer away from the edges of the pan-the flour and egg that are added help it bake up and handle beautifully.
