Banana Bread - 1 loaf

1/2 C. Shortning
1 C. Sugar
2 eggs
(cream together)
2 C. flour
1 t. soda
1 t. salt
(Sift together)
Add 1 C. mashed bananas to creamed mixture + then add dry ingredients. Bake in 350° oven for 1 hour or until done.
